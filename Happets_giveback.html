Happets donation

At Happets, we see firsthand the joy and companionship our animal friends bring to our lives. We believe it's our duty to give back and ensure a brighter future for them and generations to come. Which is why every year, we proudly donate 10% of our earnings to World Wildlife Fund (WWF) - an organization committed to conserving nature and protecting our planet's incredible biodiversity.

When you choose Happets for your beloved pets, you're not only providing them with a safe and loving environment but also actively participating in the conservation efforts of WWF. Together, we can create a future where wildlife thrives, ecosystems flourish, and the wonder of nature remains for all to enjoy.

Become a part of the Happets family and join us in this meaningful journey! With your support, we're making a positive impact—one pawprint and heartfelt donation at a time!

let's help with WWF!

The close collaboration of Happets and WWF started in 2017. By partnering with WWF, we aim to promote environmental awareness and contribute to the preservation of endangered species. We firmly believe that by working together, we can make a significant difference in protecting the habitats and ecosystems that are essential for the well-being of countless animals.

One of the primary ways we support WWF is by promoting donations on our website. We believe that every contribution, no matter how small, can have a significant impact on the lives of animals in need.  

In addition to donations, we actively promote animal adoption through our website. We believe that every animal deserves a loving and forever home. By featuring rescue and shelter animals on our platform, we hope to connect potential pet owners with their perfect companions. 

We invite you to join us on this meaningful journey. Explore WWF's website to learn more about the incredible work that WWF does and how you can contribute. Whether you choose to donate, adopt, or simply spread the word, your involvement matters. Together, we can make a significant difference and create a brighter future for animals in need.
