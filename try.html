Why Happets?

We are Happets, Macau’s most trusted and beloved pet daycare. We are a band of pet lovers and pet parents dedicated to providing your pet with a safe, fun and caring environment in your absence. We believe in a personalized approach to pet care ensuring your peace-of-mind knowing your dogs & cats are safe, happy and loved while you’re away.
