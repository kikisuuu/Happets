Dog training

Available for dogs only
Our Professional Trainers use effective techniques to teach new behaviors and proof skills. We strive to shape behavior based on trust and passion for the bond between dog and handler. Through our programs dogs thrive on learning new things and gain confidence allowing them to become happy & composed canines.


Happets offers a spacious 1,200 square foot training studio where owners and their dogs can enjoy the pleasure of dog training classes. Our training studio is equipped with a comfortable rubber flooring for extra grip and padding, fully stocked Agility equipment, Fly ball and additional professional training equipment, and large bay windows.
Play and Train:  Drop your dog off and let our trainers work with your dog on Canine Enrichment & Fitness. Choose from Agility, Basic, Intermediate, and Advanced Obedience, Tricks, Deciton (Nose Work), Rally or Flyball. Along the way your dog will learn focus and practice polite greetings and good manners with positive reinforcement. Each session is 30 minutes of one-on-one work with a professional trainer for $30.


Behaviour Problem Solving:  Getting calls about conflicts during Daycare? Ask about our Playgroup Problem Solving solutions. A certified trainer will work one on one with your dog to determine what is causing the issue and use counter conditioning to attempt to resolve the problem and get your dog back on track. Sessions are between 30-60 minutes and are $40 per session. You will receive a call or email follow up after each session.
In Home Training:  Get undivided attention and one on one training. Customized training plans crafted for your individual needs. Address common behavioral problems at the source by working privately with a trainer at your home, or work on advancing your dogâ€™s skills with Basic Obedience and Manners. Choose to have the trainer come to your home, or set up a virtual session. One-hour session $95.


Group Classes:  Join our Group Dog Training Classes held in our spacious Training Studio with rubber flooring, special equipment, natural light, and a fun and positive training space. With a maximum of 7 students per class, there is room to spread out and have sufficient working space. Puppies can get together off leash and socialize at the beginning of class. Our certified trainers will teach positive reinforcement methods in a variety of classes including Basic Obedience, Impulse control, Group Leash work and Behavior Modification classes, AKC Star Puppy classes, AKC Canine Good Citizen classes, Agility, Detection, and more. Classes meet once a week for one hour for a six week course, and the total cost is $330.
