pet sitting

Available for both cats and dogs
Pet daycare gives your pet the chance to be social in a safe daycare environment designed just for them. Under the supervision of our Certified Members, your dog will spend the day doing what they do best: playing, snacking, napping and loving life.


#1 Check-in:   Upon arrival, you and your dog are greeted by staff. If you’re both new to camp, you’ll be interviewed first to make sure your pet is a good fit for us.
#2 Group play time:  Your dog heads in with other campers and counselors to our indoor/outdoor play areas for group activity. If it’s a holiday or camper birthday, there may even be a party!


#3 Missing your pet right about now?:   Most of our locations offer petcams for our pet parents. Hop on any time and see what your best friend is up to!


#4 Nap time:   After lunch, the pets can relax, take a snooze, or play some more! Our Camp rooms have plenty of space for those who want some rest and relaxation.
#5 Extras:  We want your pet to love their days at Best Friends, so we’re big on customization. You can make sure your pet has extra cuddle time or even an ice cream with our extensive add-ons menu.


#6 Pickup:  All fun days must come to an end. When you come to get your pet, we’ll fill you in on their day—how they ate, played and slept. We’ll also let you know about any upcoming events, like scavenger hunts, ice cream socials and more.
Service start at MOP 300
