Dog walking

Available for dogs only
If you work long hours, just welcomed a new puppy into your home, or simply want your pup to get more exercise – our midday dog walking might be just for you!


Typical Visit:  We offer a variety of activities during scheduled visits including, but not limited to, feeding, fresh water, walking, running, playtime, administering medications,ear scratches and belly rubs providing treats and so much more! To keep you up to speed on the day’s activities, every visit ends with a personalized message that is emailed directly to you so you’re in the know, even when you’re on the go!
Personalized Pet Profile:  During the Initial Consultation, we take the time to get to know you and your pets and will create a Personalized Pet Profile outlining your expectations for each and every visit. Having this on hand allows our walkers and sitters to follow your typical routine, reinforce your use of commands and ensure the overall well-being and comfort of your pet while you’re away.
Service Specifics:  Our standard Midday Dog Walks allow you to schedule visits on an as-needed basis for the days you are away. Once you have completed an Initial Consultation, we are at your bark and call! Looking for a recurring weekly walk? Our discounted Monday through Friday walks, offer an affordable option allowing you to tend to your day-to-day work without having to worry about midday breaks for Fido – a little peace of mind while you’re on the go!


Our midday dog walks consist of a standard dog walking visit between the hours 10am-2pm. Specific timing requests may be accommodated based on availability. Reduced rates are offered through our contract Premiere Membership outlined below.
